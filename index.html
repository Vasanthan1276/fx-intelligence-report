ConfidenceDecision confidence combines data quality, macro coverage and agreement across the major supportive signals.
Baseline monitoring: Phase 2C.1 does not change the core scoring weights. Daily score deltas compare each new market date with the previous available market day, creating a clean baseline for the later Phase 3A evaluation.

Important: The policy bias is model-implied, not market-futures-implied. It uses the observed BIS policy-rate path together with IMF inflation and growth forecast direction, while the FX momentum layer uses recent SGD conversion-rate behaviour. Buy Urgency remains a timing aid applied only after Opportunity is considered. Singapore monetary policy is exchange-rate-centred, so the model does not invent an SGD policy rate. Retail FX rates may differ from ECB reference rates and no score guarantees future currency direction.
